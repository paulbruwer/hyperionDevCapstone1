AUTOMATION & RESTORATION

ABOUT MY HOBBY

Automation and restoration encapsulates my deep-rooted passion for all things mechanical. Inspired by my previous role as a maintenance technician for automated and semi-automated production machinery, this hobby thrives on the art of breathing new life into discarded and forgotten machines. Beyond the routine task of maintaining the intricacies of these machines, my endeavors have led me to embark on ambitious projects, restoring once-abandoned relics to their former glory. Such endeavors encompass a wide range of machines, each offering a distinct challenge and an opportunity for me to showcase my expertise. Some machines hail from a bygone era, relying on relay logic and pure mechanical prowess, while others boast the sophistication of logic computers. With unwavering dedication, I indulge in the intricate intricacies of automation and restoration, ensuring that these mechanical marvels can once again hum with the vibrant energy of their heyday.
