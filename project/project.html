C-FOLDED HAND TOWEL MACHINE

C-FOLD

I undertook a remarkable project that involved resurrecting a forsaken folded hand towel machine, which lay rusting outside on another factory's property. This fully automated machine had endured neglect and decay, with a significant section known as the finishing section missing entirely.

In addition, crucial components such as the drive motors, vacuum pump, main drive pulley, chains, timing belts, pneumatic control components and piping, timed oiling mechanism for the vacuum drums, as well as numerous relays and wiring on the electrical control side, were absent.

Determined to revive this magnificent piece of machinery, I embarked on a three-month-long restoration journey within a thriving paper factory, where this particular machine stood as the sole survivor of its kind. Meticulously, I sourced and replaced all the missing mechanical parts, employing my expertise in reverse engineering to reconstruct the intricate pneumatic and electrical control systems from scratch.

The absence of any reference for the finishing section posed a unique challenge, compelling me to design and fabricate a brand new section entirely

Furthermore, I diligently reconditioned the vacuum pump to ensure its optimal performance.

Once the machine was fully operational, my focus shifted towards seamlessly integrating it into the factory's production line. I meticulously crafted operation and maintenance guides, while also providing comprehensive training to a dedicated production team, equipping them with the necessary knowledge of operating procedures and safety protocols.

The restored folded hand towel machine now proudly serves as a valuable asset within the factory, embracing its renewed purpose with efficiency and grace.
